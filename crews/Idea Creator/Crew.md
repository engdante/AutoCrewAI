## Tasks

### Trend Analysis [Output: Market_Trends_Analysis.md]
- **Description**: Conduct a comprehensive analysis of current global markets. Identify sectors with high growth potential, low competition, and sustainability. Review economic indicators like interest rates, inflation, and consumer behavior shifts to determine where money is flowing most. Reference economic data and market news via web search if available.
- **Expected Output**: A detailed markdown report highlighting specific sectors, potential investment amounts, and projected ROI timeline. This report serves as the input for the next step.
- **Agent**: Market Analyst

### Strategy Formulation [Output: Strategy_Proposal.md]
- **Description**: Analyze the Market_Trends_Analysis.md report generated by the Market Analyst. Identify three distinct passive income streams based on the trends. For each stream, define the expected ROI, required initial capital, and maintenance requirements. Ensure the strategies align with the user's risk tolerance and capital constraints.
- **Expected Output**: A structured strategy document outlining three unique passive income streams, including estimated financial projections, implementation steps, and risk assessments.
- **Agent**: Income Strategy Architect

### Project Execution Plan [Output: Execution_Plan.md]
- **Description**: Based on the Strategy_Proposal.md, draft a step-by-step implementation guide. Include specific actions to set up the chosen passive income streams, such as listing assets, automating workflows, and managing daily operations. The plan must be actionable and time-bound.
- **Expected Output**: A comprehensive project execution plan with specific steps, timelines, and resources required for implementation.
- **Agent**: Project Implementation Specialist
